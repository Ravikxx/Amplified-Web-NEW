Rules

Read these before you join. Ignorance isn't an excuse, and "I didn't know" won't save you from a ban. When in doubt, ask staff in Discord before doing the thing.

General

1

Be respectful out of character. No targeted insults, ongoing toxicity, or public arguments. Banter is fine; harassment isn't.

2

No hate speech, discrimination, or real-life threats. Zero tolerance, permanent ban.

3

Keep content PG-13. No excessive swearing, sexual topics, extreme gore outside scripted scenes, or offensive builds, skins, or names.

4

Staff decisions on story and enforcement are final. If you disagree, appeal privately — not in public chat.

5

If you're unsure whether something is allowed, ask staff first.

In-Game

1

This is a scripted SMP. Some fights, deaths, events, and items are pre-planned. That's not a bug, it's the whole premise.

2

Don't walk into active scenes. Stay 100+ blocks away unless you're supposed to be there. If you stumble in by accident, leave immediately — don't stick around to watch.

3

Only use mods, texture packs, or clients explicitly approved by staff. Anything else is an immediate ban, no exceptions.

4

No griefing, stealing, or PvP outside of staff-approved scripted events. Even if something looks unclaimed or minor — don't touch it without permission.

5

Duped or exploited items are only allowed if staff gave them to you for a specific plot reason. Otherwise they'll be removed and you'll be punished.

6

Staged betrayals or deaths only happen when the director gives the signal. Do not go off-script.

7

Staff can reset, rewind, or rollback anything that breaks the story. Story comes first. Your progress is not exempt.

8

No spamming in chat, voice, or with commands. Includes emote spam.

Story & Roles

1

Play your assigned role to the best of your ability — ally, villain, neutral, whatever it is. Major deviations need staff approval.

2

Do not leak future plot points, scripts, or behind-the-scenes info — in-game, on Discord, or anywhere else.

3

Deaths, item losses, and betrayals may be staged. Don't assume everything is real — it's part of the story.

4

Cooperate when staff ask for a reshoot or camera angle. Getting good footage matters.

5

Unscripted chaos is only fine if it doesn't wreck a planned story beat or ruin someone else's scene.

Remember: not everyone knows what's scripted and what isn't — that ambiguity is intentional. Don't break the immersion for others.

Discord

1

Keep IC (in-character) and OOC (out-of-character) chat in the right channels. They exist for a reason.

2

No NSFW content anywhere — images, links, discussions, memes, none of it.

3

No doxxing or sharing anyone's personal information.

4

Don't spam — messages, emojis, caps, or pings. One message is enough.

5

If you're muted or banned, use the proper appeal process. Don't DM staff or post about it in public channels.

6

English only in public channels (voice and text).

Performance

1

No lag machines or excessive redstone — flying machines, large item sorters, rapid clocks, or huge auto-farms. Keep farms small and efficient.

2

No more than 30 of any single mob type in one area. Staff may cull excess mobs without warning.

3

No chunk loaders, permanent portals, or anything designed to keep lots of chunks loaded at all times.

4

If your build tanks the server, staff will ask you to simplify it. If it keeps happening, they'll remove it.

5

Report lag sources to staff instead of exploiting them.
