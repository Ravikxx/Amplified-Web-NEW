Rules

Break them and face the consequences.

Rule 1

No Hacking or Cheating

No hacked clients, X-ray, auto clickers, or any unfair advantage. Instant ban, no appeal.

Rule 2

No Griefing

Destroying other players builds is not allowed. CoreProtect logs everything. Grief gets rolled back and you get removed.

Rule 3

Respect Everyone

No racism, harassment, slurs, or targeted hate. This applies in game, Discord, and anywhere tied to the server.

Rule 4

No Spamming or Advertising

Do not spam chat or advertise other servers. This includes DMs to other players.

Rule 5

Follow Staff Instructions

If a staff member tells you to stop doing something, stop. Arguing with staff in public chat will result in a mute or kick.
